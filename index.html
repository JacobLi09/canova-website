CANOVA

Culture. Integration. Language.

Empowering Immigrant Children Through Free Education and Mentorship.

Our Vision and Mission

We believe in creating a supportive space where immigrant children can thrive, belong, and build a brighter future in Canada.

What we Offer

Mentoring

We offer free support for immigrant children by teaching them about life in Canada, helping them learn English, and providing guidance to help them feel confident and prepared in their new environment.

Tutoring

We offer free educational support in core subjects like English, math, and French to help immigrant children succeed in school. Our goal is to build strong foundations, boost confidence, and make learning fun and accessible for every child.

Homework Assistance

We provide dedicated homework support to help immigrant children stay on track with their studies. Our tutors offer guidance in subjects like English, math, and science, ensuring that students understand their assignments, improve their skills, and build confidence in their academic abilities.
